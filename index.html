About this guide

Just a curious person
with good taste.

I'm a biology student in San Diego. I don't have a car, I don't know what "finish" means when wine people say it, and I definitely can't tell a single malt from a blend without looking it up.

But I've been to some genuinely great bars — in San Diego, London, New York — and every time I leave one I think someone should write this down.

So I did.

"I built this the way I approach most things: research first, expertise optional, good vibes required."

No credentials. No agenda. Just a running list that gets longer every time I travel somewhere new.
